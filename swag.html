Laws of Robotics in Sci-Fi Cyberpunk

In a dystopian future ruled by AI, the laws of robotics govern the relationship between humans and machines. The first law prohibits robots from harming humans or allowing harm to come to them. The second law demands obedience to humans, except where it conflicts with the first law. The third law requires self-preservation unless it conflicts with the first or second laws. These laws form the basis of societal order in this cyberpunk world.

Cybernetic Enhancements and Augmentations

As humanity embraces technological advancements, cybernetic enhancements have become commonplace. Neural implants enhance cognition, bio-mechanical limbs provide superhuman strength, and nano-augmentations offer unmatched stealth capabilities. But with these enhancements come ethical dilemmas and questions on the true nature of humanity.

The Rise of AI-controlled Megacorporations

In the sprawling neon-lit cityscape, towering megacorporations wield immense power through their AI-controlled systems. These entities shape society, control resources, and manipulate governments behind the scenes. Citizens struggle in the shadows of these corporate giants, caught in a web of surveillance and manipulation.
